PHOTO #26R


R-26_______ North Burma _______ March, 1944
Sgt. Gerald T. Silvey looks on, as Sgt. Robert E. Passanisi, proceeds to repair an SCR 300. The SCR 300 was an 18 tube FM Transceiver, that was exstensively used by the Marauders, for inter unit communication. It weighted 60 Lb with its battery pack and had reliable line of sight range of twelf miles, 2 or 3 miles in the jungles.
