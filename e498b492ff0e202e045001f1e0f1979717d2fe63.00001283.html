PHOTO #14R


R-14___ North Burma ____ February - March, 1944
S/Sgt. Luther S. Player, Darlington, S. Car, reads mail from home for the first time in two months. The Marauders could get no mail while behind Jay lines for security reasons. He grins at this passage in his girl’s letter: “I’ll bet you’ re seeing plenty of action” He says he’ s going to write her, now that he’ s allowed to mail letters for the first time in three months: “Baby, you’re not kiddin”.
