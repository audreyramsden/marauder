PHOTO #21R


R-21___ North Burma ____ March, 1944
Sgt. Edward J. Eve, Sgt. Eve a bright youngster, with a gift for animals, instructed the other men of the Communication Platoon on how to pack and care for our mules.
Note: During the second campaign, Sgt., Eve was wounded with the same piece of shrapnel that hit S/Sgt. Passanisi.
