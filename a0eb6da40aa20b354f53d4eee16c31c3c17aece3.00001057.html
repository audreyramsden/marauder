H36
Capt. George P. Hickman of Pacific Palisades, California, filling plastic water bag. Plastic water bag is from an airdrop when plane parachuted good water to men surrounded at Nhpun Ga hill during 14 day battle. At the time the only water they had was from a mudhole. Hickman was a veterinarian before entering Army and is now the B Battalion Vet.
