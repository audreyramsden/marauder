PHOTO #106


MM 191 ___ North Burma ___ April-June 1944
Radio crew in 5307th. Standing, L to R, John Egan, Lt. Filiak, seated L to R, Carl Hamlic, Leon Lanphere, Chester Dulin. These Marauders marched all day, set up equipment at end of march and operated radio all night, handling messages vital to the success and survival of the 5307th.
