The Merrill's Marauders 59th ANNUAL REUNION - 2005
WILL BE HELD IN WASHINGTON D.C. AT THE HYATT REGENCY HOTEL, CRYSTAL CITY ARLINGTON VIRGINIA
Friday, Saturday, Sunday. AUGUST 26, 27 & 28

All Marauders, The 475th Inf., The 124th Cal., Mars Task Force, Supports Units,
Friends and Relatives are All Invited.

The Hyatt Regency Crystal City Hotel just outside of our Nation's Capital in Arlington Virginia, will serve as venue for the 59th annual reunion of the Merrill's Marauders Association. The hotel offers complimentary shuttle service to Reagan International Airport, the Metro (Washington subway) and nearby shopping centers. Important to note, that the corrected dates are, Friday, the 26th of August, through Sunday, the 28th of August. A tour is planed to the new World War II Memorial, the Japanese American Memorial and other historical sites. The Marauders will also place a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and visit the nearby Merrill's Marauder Memorial.

 The Package price for the 2005 reunion, including tax and gratuity, will be

$330 for a double (2 per. room)
$250 for a single (1 per. room)
$410 for a triple (3 per. room)
$490 for a quadruple ( 4 per room)

 The package rate includes, two (2) nights lodging, Friday Night Dinner, Saturday Breakfast, a Ladies Tea, Saturday Night Dinner and Sunday Breakfast.
The hotel rate for extra night stays, is $79.00 plus tax per night.
