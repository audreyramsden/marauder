                The State of Tennessee has Proclaimed September 4,1999

                "Merrill's Marauders Day"

                

                The Governor of the state of Tennessee, Don Sundquist, has

                Proclaimed September 4, 1999, as "Merrill's Marauders Day"

                in the state of Tennesse to honor the courage and valor these

                brave veterans of World War II, and encourage all citizens to

                join him in this worthy observance.
