PHOTO #118


MM 216 ___ North Burma ___ Feb-June, 1944
The 5307th marched and fought for five months on an unvarying diet of K Ration. Stilwell's Chinese Forces would not accept this diet. They were airdropped peanut oil, fresh vegetables, rice, canned meats and fruits. During the campaign when paths crossed a lively market developed Marauders bartering for real food.
