PHOTO #H29


H29___March 3, 1944___La Gang Ga
1st. Lt. Dominic (Pat) Perrone, holding the long bayonet and group of Marauders, standing over remains of a Jap patrol that was ambushed by Perrone’s Platoon. Perrone had a sixth sense and could tell when Japanese were in the area, his sixth sense was instrumental in spotting the Japs and setting up the ambush.
