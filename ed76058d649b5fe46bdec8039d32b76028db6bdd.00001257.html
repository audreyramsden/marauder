PHOTO #1R




R-1 ___ North Burma ____ February 1944
Marauders crossing a tributary of the Chidwin River, one the main waterways in Northern Burma L to R, Unknown, Pfc. Philip Morrissey, Pfc. Joseph Calaci. Phil Morrissey was Lead Scout for B Battalion when the enemy was encountered on trail through Elephant Grass. He fired first killing two Japanese. He is credited with getting first blood for B Battalion
