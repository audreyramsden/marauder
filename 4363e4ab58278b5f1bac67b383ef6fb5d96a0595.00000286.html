I'm searching for more specific service information-as well as anyone who might have served with or known my dad. I do have more service information, but someone might remember him more for his stories-getting thrown in the brig for refusing to do patrol duty because of "man-eating tigers," wandering around India because he got seperated from his unit, etc. etc. I'll share what I know if you'll share with me!
